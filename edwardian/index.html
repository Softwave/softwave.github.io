Edwardianiser

A tool for writers of period fiction, made with Express.js, the openAI API and the Rivet AI programming environment. Particularly I had players of the Titanic Voyage RPG in mind, when putting this together. Note that, like anya AI tool, the Edwardianiser is better used as a rought guide than as an all-encompassing solution.

The app is accessible here. Add it to your homescreen on iOS (tutorial) and it functions properly as a mobile app.
